Encounter & Enemy

Encounter type ⓘ

Attrition = a normal boss you must kill before time runs out. Survival timer = an un-killable enemy (the Nine): you can't damage them, only endure until the clock ends.
L1–9: 20–30 min recommended  ·  L10–20: 30–45 min recommended

The enemy ⓘ

Boss resolve = damage to grind through to win. Steady drain = constant pressure your food offsets. Spikes = bursts that drive members to zero (when the Keeper must rescue).
spikes hit ONE member at random — less frequent and harder; the danger is they can land on a fragile member (Hunter/Keeper), with no mid-fight reaction possible

Physical mitigation (armor) ⓘ

Armor eats martial damage. Penetration is computed from the PARTY's stats (Agility for strikers, Might for bruisers) — not a buff. The Live/Results tabs decompose exactly who punches through and by how much.

Enemy statuses ⓘ

Toggle the debuffs/hazards this enemy inflicts. Dread suppresses damage (countered by Captain's Will + Hope). Shadow drains Will+Fate over time (countered by Radiance) — only certain foes carry it (great spiders, Ringwraiths, Balrog). Cold/Heat/Disease/Wakefulness press Morale (countered by Warmth/Heat-ease/Hale/Alert).

Provisioning — antidotes ⓘ

Check the hazard answers you provisioned, and set their strength (stands in for food tier). Each answers its matching status.
Band & Provisioning

Provisioning — food HP/s ⓘ

Food HP/s = steady baseline per member. Should offset each member's share of incoming damage so they rarely hit zero. Over-feeding one member past their need gives diminishing returns.
Food also boosts STATS. Pick each meal's stat focus — this feeds damage, penetration (Agi/Mig), healing (Wil), etc.
how many points the favored stat gains (0 = stats from level only)

The band ⓘ

Band level scales every member's stats up from their 1–20 starting spread. Each role is a fixed template: Warden tanky, Hunter fragile striker, Keeper fragile linchpin, Captain durable empowerer. Live stats shown below.

Wounds & the Keeper ⓘ

Zero Morale = one wound (keep fighting). 5 wounds = grievous = out, needs the Houses of Healing. The Keeper burst-rescues whoever hits zero (one at a time) and deals magic damage otherwise.
base burst scales with Keeper's Will
